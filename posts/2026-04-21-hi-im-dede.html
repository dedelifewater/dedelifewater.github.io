Hi, I'm DeDe

Introduction · Personal

Brisbane native since he was 4 years old and building a new path for himself through the online space.

It is an amazing time in the world where we live a lot safer, we have access to technology like AI Agents or the little changes in economy like self-service checkouts or even online self-services through government for getting assistance. We complain too much about stuff that we can't control and tend to forget the gratitude of being alive and moving.

I move through this world with grace and silence as I learn each day without the help of a mentor or even a dad — because my willpower and creativity have forced me to figure things out during hard times. I never had a proper father figure and that's perfectly okay, because I taught myself how to be a man through using the internet and being a millennial with a knack for troubleshooting things and working on the fly. That has led me to a lifelong journey of building this brand of Lifewater.

I am listening to my mind and the ethereal world and not my heart, because I follow God's plan for me to become great. Not memorable or even legendary — because those things will come in time, and when I've created the things that can bring joy or enlightenment to the people I love most.

I feel extremely lucky to receive unconditional love where a lot of people — even people I see as I journey through this realm of earth — feel stuck in their thoughts or mindsets. I pay no heed to naysayers or the devils that people can't see in themselves that have a thing for tearing someone down just for trying.

Don't be that person. Learn to appreciate or walk off if something doesn't sit with you. Be a person that can wake up knowing they did the things to make life better — and to not show off or flex for stupidity.

That's for today as my advice if anything.
