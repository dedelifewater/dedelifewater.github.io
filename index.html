Who Is DeDe Lifewater

Born from the philosophy of water — adaptive, persistent, and life-giving. DeDe Lifewater is a rapper and writer carving out an authentic space, guided by the belief that growth is the only constant.

Drawing from conscious lyricism, personal experience, and the art of becoming, this is a journey told in bars and prose. No shortcuts. No performance. Just the honest current.
